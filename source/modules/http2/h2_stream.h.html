25/**
26 * A HTTP/2 stream, e.g. a client request+response in HTTP/1.1 terms.
27 * 
28 * A stream always belongs to a h2_session, the one managing the
29 * connection to the client. The h2_session writes to the h2_stream,
30 * adding HEADERS and DATA and finally an EOS. When headers are done,
31 * h2_stream is scheduled for handling, which is expected to produce
32 * h2_headers/RESPONSE buckets.
33 *
34 * The h2_headers may be followed by more h2_headers (interim responses) and
35 * by DATA frames read from the h2_stream until EOS is reached. Trailers
36 * are send when a last h2_headers is received. This always closes the stream
37 * output.
38 */ view japanese

 * ストリームは常に h2_session に属しており、クライアントへの接続を管理します。

 * これにより、常にストリームの出力が終了します。
